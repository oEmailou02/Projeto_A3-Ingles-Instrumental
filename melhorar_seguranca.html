What are the types of digital security?

As we said, digital security encompasses everything that protects the digital environment, its systems and information. This set, however, is made up of more specific types of digital security, each with its own purpose and application. Discover some of them below.

Network security

It is the type of security focused on protecting an organization's networks. It includes firewalls, intrusion detection and prevention systems, and VPNs (virtual private networks).

Application security

Here, as the name suggests, the focus is on a company's applications and systems.

Endpoint Security

The name in English means that the focus of this type of security is the equipment used by the company's users, such as laptops, smartphones and tablets.

Cloud security

As the name suggests, It is security focused on systems and data that are in the cloud.

Access identity management

Here security aims to control and manage access to a company's systems and data.

Information security

This is what guarantees the confidentiality and integrity of data stored in digital environments and transferred from one place to another.

How important is digital security?

Digital security helps protect people and companies from viruses, data theft, cyber attacks and unauthorized access to networks, cell phones, tablets and information systems.
Therefore, digital security is essential to guarantee the integrity and confidentiality of the tons of data that are stored and that travel from one place to another.
